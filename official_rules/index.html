Official Rules
1. There will absolutely be no quitting. I repeat absolutely no quitting. If you’re having a issue come up, pause the game and get in touch with your opponent immediately
2. Broadcasting The away team will broadcast every game, so the home team can have their home field advantage.
3. Trading All trades must be approved by 3 or more commissioners. There would be no cpu trading at all. No cpu trading at all.
4. There will be an 48 hours advancement period. Advancing will be at 8am EASTERN STANDARD TIME TIME. AND ALSO THERE IS NO FORCE WINS AT ALL IN KING MADDEN LEAGUE, IF YOU ARE PLAYING A CPU TEAM THEN YOU MUST PLAY THAT GAME BUT ONLY GET 1 START AT IT!! ALSO YOU WILL HAVE 24 HOURS TO SCHEDULE GAMES OR YOU WILL BE ISSUED A WARNING
5. Fourth Down Rule Fourth and 1/inches u can go for it if your pass your own 35 yard line Pass the 50 yard line, u can go for it anytime. You can fake punt at any time. *if you are down more than 2 tds(15+) you may go for it on fourth.
6.THREE MEN MUST RUSH THE QUARTERBACK AT ALL TIMES. **SAME PLAY RULE U can not run the same play back to back
7.Must mix up your offensive & defensive plays
8. 4th QTR If you’re losing at anytime in the fourth quarter u can go for it at anytime. We’re making a coach decision to go for it in the fourth quarter. Example: if you’re up and u don’t want to give the ball back to your opponent because of momentum has swung.
9. Position ChangesHave to put in a request for position changes Violations Rules 1st violation
10. Warning ⚠️ 2nd violation - 2 games suspension 3rd violation - 3 games suspension 4th violation - Banned from league
